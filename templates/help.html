How to Use JournAI

Writing an Entry

From the dashboard, choose "Make an Entry". You can either write for today or select a custom date from the weekly calendar view. The editor allows you to write using Markdown for formatting, such as using `#` for headers or `*` for italics and bold.

Understanding Memories

Your memories give your AI companion context about your life. Click the brain icon () in the header to open them. There are two types:

  • Your Memories: This is your space. Add core facts about yourself, your life, and your preferences (e.g., "My best friend's name is Sarah."). The AI treats this as the absolute truth.
  • AI Learned: As you write, the AI will pick up on new details (like the name of a pet or a new hobby) and add them here automatically. You can review, edit, or delete these.

The Journal View

This is your timeline. You can navigate between weeks using the arrows. Days with entries are filled in, while empty days are a prompt to write. Click any day to read or write an entry for that date.
